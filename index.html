Castling

Daily Puzzle

How to Play

  1. Tap anywhere on the board to place your rook.
  2. Tap on a square (or use the arrow keys) to move to it.
  3. You can only move by the number of squares shown on the square you're currently on.
  4. Land on every square exactly once, then return to the position you started from, to win.

Tips

  • Tap your rook to undo your last move. You can undo as often as you like.
  • A new daily puzzle appears every day at midnight UTC.
  • Every puzzle has a solution (some puzzles have multiple solutions).
  • Every puzzle can be solved no matter which square you start from.
  • Keyboard shortcuts:
    • ←, →, ↑, and ↓ to move.
    • Z to undo.
    • If your rook isn't on the board or hasn't moved yet, S to start from a random square.
    • 1, 2, and 3 to select the Regular, Advanced, and Master puzzles, respectively.
